Our Process

How Our Power Washing Process Works

1

Property Inspection

We assess surface materials, staining, and structural condition before any equipment is set up or deployed.

2

Pre-Treatment

Biodegradable cleaning solutions are applied to break down mold, mildew, grease, and organic growth before washing.

3

Pressure or Soft-Wash

Correct PSI and nozzle selection for each surface type. We never blast wood or soft siding at high pressure.

4

Final Rinse & Review

Clean rinse pass, full walkthrough, and before-and-after photos provided on request.
