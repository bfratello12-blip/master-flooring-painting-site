Laminate — Classic Look, Modern Durability

A long-time favorite for living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways, laminate delivers the warm look of hardwood with a tough, easy-clean surface that stands up to daily life.

Subfloor Preparation

The Foundation of a Great Floor Starts Below

Even the best flooring fails prematurely if the subfloor is not properly prepared. We flatten, clean, seal, and inspect your subfloor to manufacturer specifications before any material goes down — eliminating squeaks, soft spots, and moisture issues before they become expensive problems.

  • Leveling, patching, and flatness correction
  • Moisture barrier and vapor retarder installation
  • Old flooring removal and responsibly disposed
  • Squeak elimination and structural spot repairs
  • Hardwood installation-ready substrate prep

How Our Flooring Installation Process Works

1

Free Consultation
