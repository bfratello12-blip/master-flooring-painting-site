Deck Prep

Proper Prep Before Painting or Staining

Long-lasting deck coatings start with prep. We remove buildup, loose coatings, and weathered fibers before any stain or paint is applied.

  • Deck washing to remove algae and dirt
  • Loose coating removal and sanding
  • Nail and board inspection before coating
  • Prep for rails, steps, and spindles
  • Dry-time planning before finish coats

Our Process

How Our Deck Painting and Staining Process Works

1

Inspection & Estimate
